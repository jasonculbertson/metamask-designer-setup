Xcode Required
Xcode includes the iOS Simulator needed to run MetaMask. It's free and takes about 10 minutes to install.
🍺
Homebrew Required
Homebrew is a free package manager that installs developer tools. Open Terminal and paste this command:
/bin/bash -c "$(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Homebrew/install/HEAD/install.sh)"
click to copy
It will ask for your Mac password. When it finishes, come back here and click Continue.
